How We Work

From Seed to Plantation-Ready

Our structured 9–12 month nursery programme ensures every seedling meets the highest standards of health, vigour and genetic purity.

📦
1
Seed Receipt
Seeds verified against D/O. Damage checked within 5% tolerance. HQ audit conducted.
🌱
2
Pre-Nursery (TSK)
Seeds planted in germination trays with per-tray records and empty hole counts.
🌿
3
Main Nursery (TSB)
Healthy seedlings transplanted to polybags. 1st culling conducted — target <10% removal.
✂️
4
Culling & Grading
2nd and 3rd culling with drone imagery verification and DIA data recorded per plot.
🚛
5
Delivery
Plantation-ready seedlings transferred with full SIN documentation and drone mapping.
